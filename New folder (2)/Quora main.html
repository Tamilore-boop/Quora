Welcome to Quora

Quora is a website aimed at helping young teenagers to become what they want to become in life with guidelines. it was developed and inspired by a boy who had a strong desire for success. in this website various ways of assisting students will be provided below.

Aims of Quora:

  • To help students develop a brighter future
  • To encourage students
  • To help students work on their weaknesses
  • To guide students on the right path
  • To prepare students for both internal and external exams

How to make progress in academics

Step 1: Study regularly

it is recommended that you always study and you should always devote time for your books, review your daily work after school and try to answer questions related to it, this way what you have just learnt earlier in the day will stick to your brain and if there are any questions you have issues with, you can reach out to your teacher.

Step 2: Listen to your teachers

When your teachers are teaching you, it is very important to pay attention to whatever your teachers are saying so that you have a good understanding of what they are teaching you. Most times during lectures important points are given and explained to you.

Step 3: Do your class work

After lectures and giving appropriate number of examples, cllassworks should be given to you to test your knowledge on what you have learnt and you should try your very best to answer the questions and give your teacher to review it for you.

Step 4: Do your corrections

When you do your classworks and you did not get some right, go to your teachers and ask them for corrections so that you willl n ot make the same mistake next time when you are solving a question like that.

Step 5: Ask questions in the class

Ask your teachers questions if you are taught a topic and you don't get it, feel free to ask them, they are always there for you and you have paid for iit so do not be shy or scared to ask questions.

Step 6: Choose your friends wisely

Evil association corrupts good manners they say, if you want to perform well in your academics ensure that you roll with responsible students and not the ones that have bad attitude to schoool work and education.

Step 7: Study during free periods

Instead of playing during free periods, you can go to the library and research about some new things you have learnt or study about them so that you can have a better understanding of it and be a step higher than your classmates.

step 8: Never disobey your teachers

Always obey your teachers and do things that are right every time even if your teachers are present or absent, Do your classwork unfailingly and follow their instructions.


Things to do at home

Step 1: Review what you have learnt

After your day at school, shower, take a break and review what you have learnt in school that day, try more questions and meet your friends to work together in solving questions.

Step 2: Write down any difficulties encountered during your study

If you study at home and there are some things you do not understand, make sure you get to your teacher in school and explain any difficulties you might have encountered while studying so that your teacher who has better understanding of it can put you through.

Step 3: Pray always

Always pray to God to crown your efforts before and after you study so.
